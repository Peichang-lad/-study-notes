$y = (1+x)^{\frac{1}{x}}$ 在 $x \to 0$ 时的展开
$$ (1+x)^{\frac{1}{x}} = e - \frac{e}{2}x + \frac{11e}{24}x^2 + o(x^2) $$
$y = (1+\frac{1}{x})^x$ 在 $x \to \infty$ 时的展开
$$ (1+\frac{1}{x})^x = e - \frac{e}{2x} + \frac{11e}{24x^2} + o\left(\frac{1}{x^2}\right) $$
